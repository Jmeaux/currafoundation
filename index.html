what we do

Curra Foundation is a low-cost transportation company that provides reliable and affordable transportation to families residing in low-income communities who need to visit their loved ones incarcerated in correctional facilities in Northern California.

Evident

As evidenced by the success of Curra's sustainable transportation system, providing a low-cost transportation solution for families in need does not have to come at a premium cost.

Costs

With daily transportation costs being a significant financial burden for low-income families worldwide, Curra Foundation is committed to mitigating this burden by offering affordable and reliable transportation to families in low-income communities while recognizing the need for sustainable and cost-effective infrastructure.

Inspiration

Drawing inspiration from the case of the public transport system in Curra, our transportation proposal acknowledges and responds to users' needs.

services

In keeping with the successful and cost-effective model of the Curra Foundation transportation system, the Curra Foundation seeks to provide a transportation solution that is accessible and affordable to families in need.

MISSION STATEMENT

Curra Foundation is dedicated to fostering strong familial and community bonds by providing reliable transportation services for families yearning to connect with incarcerated loved ones. Through our commitment to exceptional service, we aim to address the challenges and limitations accompanying the inflexible penal regime and rigidity of prison life. Moreover, our mission extends beyond providing reliable transportation services.

Vision

Our vision at Curra Foundation is to empower communities and bridge the distance that separates families from their loved ones through our reliable transportation services. Our commitment is to ensure that families remain connected regardless of physical distance, economic disparities, or medical conditions.

About Curra

By designing a transportation system that is both accessible and reliable, Curra Foundation aims to reduce the financial burden low-income families face by providing them with an affordable means of transportation to visit their incarcerated loved ones.
